SITE SPECIFIC

Like many other people, I walk everywhere. So many times am I walking on the side walk and a group of people are walking 3 wide or not paying attention causing me to get pushed off of the sidewalk. One of my pet peeves is people that are not aware of how they walk and how it affects others. I needed a way to make an impact and alert those who might not realize what they are doing. It needed to be something so big that it caused people to look up, pay attention and even merge into single file when walking with a group. The goal of this installation was to show people what they do when they walk.
